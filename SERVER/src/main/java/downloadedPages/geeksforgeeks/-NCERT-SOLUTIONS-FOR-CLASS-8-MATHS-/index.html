    Looking for NCERT Solutions for Class 8 Maths that will solve your maths problems in a jiffy? Dont worry because your search ends here. GeeksforGeeks have Maths solutions to all your Maths questions.

    NCERT Solutions for Class 8 Maths consists of the chapter-wise solutions to all the problems provided in the NCERT textbook for Class 8 Mathematics. GeeksforGeeks has created a detailed chapter-wise solution for the NCERT book of class 8 that contains problems on various topics like Rational Numbers, Linear Equations, Quadrilaterals, Data Handling, and many more. Each chapter in this solution thoroughly covers every exercise along with a detailed step-by-step explanation of the solutions.

    NCERT Solutions for Class 8 Maths are incredibly useful for students studying for CBSE exams as well as state or national competitive tests like Olympiads. The CBSE examinations adhere to the structure established by the NCERT texts. 

    There are 16 chapters altogether in this book. As a student, you must constantly revise and practice all of the solutions. Begin by answering the problems in the NCERT book, and then check at these class 8 maths NCERT solutions. 

    To improve the basic knowledge of students, we have also covered 1500+ Most asked Questions of Mathematics, Chapterwise Important Formulas, and many more based on the new Class 8th CBSE curriculum. These notes and other study materials provide a helping hand for students to prepare for their Final Examinations.

    Chapter 1: Rational Numbers

    This chapter helps to learn about the properties of real numbers, rational numbers, the representation of a rational number on a number line, and additive and multiplicative identity. Here in NCERT solutions for class 8 maths chapter 1, we have covered NCERT Problems based on Rational numbers mainly discussing the characteristics of all the real numbers, integers, whole numbers, rational numbers, and natural numbers. 

    This chapter consists of two exercises only in which the problems in Exercise 1.1 are related to the properties of the rational numbers (closure, commutativity, associativity, etc). However, in Exercise 1.2 the problems are related to the advanced concepts of the rational number like the representation of rational numbers on a number line and determining any numbers of rational numbers between any two rational numbers.

    Chapter 2: Linear Equations in One Variable

    The linear equations in one variable deal with the expression defined in one variable only and its algebraic operations. This chapter contains six different exercises that have problems based on the linear equations in one variable and their application. 

    There are 65 well-structured questions in Chapter 2 linear equations in one variable, which may be divided into 20 simple calculation sums, 38 intermediate levels, and 7 difficult problems. Exercises 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 are designed to determine the solution of the linear equation. However, Exercises 2.5 and 2.6 are based on the topic equations reducible to the linear form.

    Chapter 3: Understanding Quadrilaterals

    This chapter covers all types of quadrilaterals such as polygonal shapes like squares, rectangles, triangles, pentagons, hexagons, etc. 

    In total, this chapter contains four exercises that include a total of 31 questions, out of which 7 are easy, 16 are medium and 8 are difficult problems. Exercise 3.1 covers the problems on the definition of various polygons and their properties, Exercise 3.2 is based on the concept of the Angle sum property of a polygon. However, Exercise 3.3 covers the elements and the properties of quadrilaterals like trapezium, kite, and parallelogram, and Exercise 3.4 is designed to learn some special types of parallelogram like square, rectangle, and rhombus.

    Chapter 4: Practical Geometry

    The chapter on practical geometry helps to learn the construction of quadrilaterals when different parameters of it are known. Geometry may be visualized and realized in the actual world with the help of practical geometry. This can be applied in architecture, sculpture, dimension calculations, and other areas.

    This chapter contains a total of five exercises only, with 8 questions in total. Each question is divided into 3-4 subparts with easy, to difficult questions. Exercise 4.1 covers the problem for the case when the lengths of four sides and a diagonal are given. Similarly, Exercises 4.2, 4.3, and 4.4 are based on the topics when two diagonals and three sides are known, two adjacent sides and three angles are given and three sides and two included angles are provided. However, Exercise 4.5 contains problems based on some special cases.

Exercise 5.1 is based on the basic concept of representing, organizing, and grouping the data provided while Exercise 5.2 helps to make a pie chart for the given data.     Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    Question 1: What are some important formulas from Class 8 Maths NCERT?

    Answer:

    Here is the list of some important formulas covered in NCERT solutions from Mensuration and Algebra, for class 8 maths are given below:

    Important Mensuration formulas:

    • TSA of Cuboid = 2(lb + bh +lh)
    • TSA of Cube = 6a2
    • TSA of Right Circular Cone = r (l + r)
    • CSA of Solid Shapes
    • CSA of Cuboid = 2(l + b)
    • CSA of Cube = 4a2
    • CSA of Right Circular Cone = rl
    • Volume of Cuboid = lbh
    • Volume of Cube = a3
    • Volume of Right Circular Cone = r2h
