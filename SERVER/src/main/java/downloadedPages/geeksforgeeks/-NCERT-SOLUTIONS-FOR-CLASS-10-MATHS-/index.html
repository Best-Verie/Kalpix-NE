    Chapter 1: Real Numbers

    The chapter covers NCERT Solutions for class 10 maths chapter 1 – Real numbers which vastly explains the concept of properties of real numbers using Euclids Lemma, Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic. 

    The first chapter of Class 10 maths has a total of 18 questions, 10 of which are simple, 5 moderate, and 3 of which are lengthy answer-type problems. The problems in Exercises 1.1 and 1.2 are needed to find out the divisibility of integers using Euclids division algorithm and the factorization of composite numbers using the fundamental theorem of arithmetic. However, Exercise 1.3 and 1.4 includes the question to prove the roots of any irrational number and decimal expansions of rational numbers.

Exercises 2.1 and 2.2 consist of problems that need to determine the number of zeroes through a graph and the relation between zeroes and coefficients of a polynomial. The problems in Exercises 3.3 and 3.4 are to be solved using the substitution and elimination methods. Rest exercises 3.5, 3.6, and 3.7 include the questions from the applications of the linear pair of any linear equations in two variables.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    Question 1: What are some important formulas from Class 10 Maths NCERT?

    Answer:

    Here is the list of some important formulas covered in NCERT solutions from Algbra, trigonometry, and circles, for class 10 maths are given below:

    Important Algebraic Formulas are-

    • (a + b)2 = a2 + b2 + 2ab
    • (a + b) (a-b) = a2 – b2
    • (a – b)2 = a2 + b2 – 2ab

    Formulas of Arithmetic Progressions are-

    • nth term = a + (n-1) d
    • Sum upto nth term = n/2 [2a + (n – 1) d]

    Important Trigonometric Formulas are-

    • sin2 + cos2 = 1
    • cosec2 = 1 + cot2 
    • sec2 = 1 + tan2 

    Formulas for Circles are-

    • Circumference = 2r
    • Area = r2
